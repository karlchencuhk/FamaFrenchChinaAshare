Stkcd [Stock Code] - Stock code released by the exchange of the listed company
ShortName_EN [Stock Short Name] - Stock Short Name released by the exchange of the listed company.
Accper [Ending Date of Statistics] - Date of accounting statement, denoted by 10 characters, e.g., 1999-12-31.
Typrep [Statement Type] - The listed company's financial statements are consolidated statements or parent statements. A=Consolidated Statements, B=Parent Statements.
A003100000 [Total Equity Attributable to Owners of the Parent Company] - The portion of equity attributable to owners of the parent in consolidated statements. Used from 2007 onwards.
A003000000 [Total Shareholders’ Equity] - The sum of all shareholders' equity items. Used from 1990 onwards.
